Powerful Features

Discover all the amazing features that make Quizlr the perfect platform for creating and managing your quizzes.

User Login

Secure user authentication system with personalized dashboards for each user. Save your quiz progress, access your quiz history, and track your performance over time. Customize your profile and manage your quiz preferences. Option to login with social media accounts or email.

Collaborative Quiz Creation

Create and edit quizzes together with team members in real-time. Share question banks and templates within your organization. Set different access levels for viewers, editors, and administrators. Track all changes with detailed revision history and restore previous versions if needed.

Dark and Light Theme

Switch seamlessly between dark and light modes to suit your preference and reduce eye strain. Automatically detect system preferences for theme selection. Customize accent colors and contrast levels for better accessibility. Consistent design across both themes for a seamless experience.

Certificate of Completion

Generate professional certificates upon successful quiz completion. Customize certificate templates with your branding and quiz details. Download certificates in multiple formats (PDF, PNG). Include unique verification codes and QR codes for authenticity verification.

Real-time Analytics Dashboard

Monitor quiz performance with comprehensive analytics in real-time. View detailed statistics including completion rates, average scores, and time spent. Generate visual reports with charts and graphs for better insights. Export analytics data in various formats for further analysis.
